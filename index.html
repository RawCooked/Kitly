Why Kitly?

Everything you need,
nothing you don't.

Kitly is designed to be simple, fast, and powerful. Set up your entire machine in under a minute.

Lightning Fast

Install 20+ apps with a single command. Kitly handles everything in parallel using Winget's engine.

Curated Bundles

Pre-built bundles for developers, gamers, creators, and sysadmins. Or create your own custom bundles.

Safe & Open Source

100% open source. Every package is installed directly through Microsoft's official Winget repository.

Fully Extensible

Create, share, and import bundles. Your packages.json is your portable setup — take it anywhere.

Developer First

Built by developers, for developers. Clean CLI interface with intuitive commands you already know.

Stay Updated

Update all your apps with kitly update. Keep every tool in your bundle at the latest version.

About the Creator

Built with passion.

Kitly was created by a Computer Science Engineering student specialized in Artificial Intelligence who was tired of spending hours reinstalling the same applications on every new machine.

The goal was simple: build a tool that makes setting up a Windows machine as effortless as it should be. No GUIs, no clicking through 15 installers — just one command and you're done.

Kitly is free, open source, and community-driven. Contributions, bundle suggestions, and feedback are always welcome.
